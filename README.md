# 🌸 The Ultimate Mother's Day Digital Greeting Card

Want to give your mom a gift that stands out? Skip the paper card and build her a website!

Created by Aarav Bhati, this open-source HTML template is the perfect way to show your appreciation. It’s fully responsive, beautifully designed, and entirely customizable. Best of all? You can feature a personalized photo of your mom right in the center of the page!

✨ Features:

📸 Custom Photo Integration: Easily swap the placeholder image with your favorite picture of your mom.

⚡ Lightweight & Fast: Pure HTML/CSS. No heavy frameworks or complicated setups.

📱 Fully Responsive: Looks perfect on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

🚀 Ready to Deploy: Host it instantly on platforms like Netlify or GitHub Pages in seconds!

🛠️ How to Use It (In 3 Easy Steps):

Clone/Download this repository.

Replace the image.jpg file in the folder with a picture of your mom.

Deploy the folder live and send her the link!

If you use this template, give the repo a ⭐ and share your creations!
